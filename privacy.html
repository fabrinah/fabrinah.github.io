Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who is responsible for your data

This website is operated by Fabrinah, a freelance Data Entry & AI Data Annotation specialist. For any privacy-related questions, you can reach me via the contact form on this site.

What data is collected and why

When you submit the contact form, the following information is collected:

  • Name — to address you in my reply
  • Email address — to respond to your message
  • Message content — to understand your request

This data is collected solely to respond to your inquiry. It is not used for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.

How your data is processed

Messages submitted through the contact form are transmitted via Web3Forms, a third-party form service. Web3Forms processes form submissions on my behalf and forwards them to my email inbox. Web3Forms acts as a data processor under the GDPR. You can review their privacy policy at web3forms.com/privacy.

No other third parties receive your personal data.

How long data is kept

Your data is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your request, and in any case no longer than 12 months. After that period, messages are deleted.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access the data held about you
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in France: CNIL)

To exercise any of these rights, please use the contact form on this site.

Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technology. No analytics or advertising scripts are loaded.
